About Me
👨🏽‍💻 Product Designer based in Bengaluru, India.
🖋️ My design journey began back in my sophomore year of college, where curiosity led me to start crafting interfaces, and passion kept me going. I’m a self-taught designer who believes in building not just functional experiences, but meaningful ones that feel natural and intuitive.
❤️ Over the past 3+ years, I’ve worked across startups and teams like Recotap, Ziroh Labs, Diamondpick (as a freelance designer), and UnQ Technologies (as an Intern). I’ve been lucky to work on everything from design systems and product revamps to full-scale 0→1 experiences, leading visual and UX direction while collaborating closely with cross-functional teams.
💡 I thrive in fast-paced environments where ideas are quick, iterations are faster, and learning never stops. Whether it’s simplifying complex workflows, shaping thoughtful interactions, or crafting a cohesive brand, I aim to deliver clarity through design.
🚀 Outside of design, I’m equally passionate about music production & Singing, experimenting as a would-be chef, and living a disciplined life around fitness. If I hadn’t become a designer, you’d probably find me music artist, plating gourmet meals, or training someone at a gym.
